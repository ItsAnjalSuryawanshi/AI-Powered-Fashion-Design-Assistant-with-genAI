Week 1: Fashion Domain Research Summary
Intern: Anjal Suryawanshi
Project: AI-Powered Fashion Design Assistant with Generative Models


Part 1: Fashion Design Workflow (Sketch to Garment)
Stage 1: Concept & Inspiration
What happens: Designer researches trends, creates mood boards, selects color palettes

AI Application: Generate mood boards using Stable Diffusion with text prompts like "summer dress pastel colors bohemian style"

Stage 2: Sketching
What happens: Draw fashion flats (technical sketches) using croquis templates

AI Application: ControlNet for pose-guided sketch generation from text descriptions

Stage 3: Technical Drawing
What happens: Add measurements, stitch details, fabric notes to flat sketches

AI Application: DeepFashion dataset landmarks (shoulder, neckline points) for garment alignment

Stage 4: Pattern Making
What happens: Create paper/digital patterns for each garment piece

AI Application: AI generates pattern templates from 2D sketches

Stage 5: Fabric Selection
What happens: Choose materials (cotton, silk, denim) based on drape, texture, season

AI Application: ChromaDB vector retrieval for fabric texture recommendations

Stage 6: Sample Creation
What happens: Sew first prototype garment

AI Application: Generate fabric visualization on body using diffusion models

Stage 7: Fitting & Alterations
What happens: Fit on model, adjust measurements, pin for alterations

AI Application: Pose-based garment fitting prediction using ControlNet

Stage 8: Production
What happens: Mass manufacturing, quality control, packaging

AI Application: Automated quality inspection using computer vision

Key Insight for AI Assistant: At each stage, the AI should suggest: style options, color combinations, fabric pairings, and design improvements based on trending data.
